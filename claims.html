Claims & Support

Follow these steps to raise a claim or contact our claims team.

  1. Report: Call our 24/7 claims helpline 1800-123-456 or use the form below.
  2. Upload documents: Police FIR (motor/theft), hospital bills, invoices or photos.
  3. Inspection: For motor/home claims we arrange an assessor visit or virtual inspection.
  4. Settlement: Cashless repair / reimbursement as per policy terms.
