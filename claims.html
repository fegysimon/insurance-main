How to file a claim

We know that making a claim usually means you’re going through a difficult time. Our goal is to keep the process simple and transparent.

Simple 4-step process

Step 1
Notify us

Inform us about the incident within 24 hours (for health/motor) or as soon as reasonably possible (for life/home).

  • Call our 24×7 helpline: 1800-123-456
  • Email: claims@securelife.insure
Step 2
Submit documents

Our team will share a simple checklist based on the type of claim. Common documents include:

  • Policy copy and ID proof
  • Hospital records / FIR / Death certificate (as applicable)
  • Bank details for claim payout
Step 3
Assessment

We review your documents and may ask for clarifications if required. Most claims are assessed within 3–7 working days after receiving complete documents.

Step 4
Settlement

Once approved, the claim amount is transferred directly to the registered bank account. You’ll receive SMS and email updates at every step.

Cashless health claims

For planned or emergency hospitalisation at network hospitals:

  • Show your e-health card at the hospital help desk.
  • Hospital will send us a pre-authorisation request.
  • We coordinate directly with the hospital for payments as per policy terms.

Motor insurance claims

After an accident or damage:

  • Ensure everyone is safe and call emergency services if needed.
  • Inform us before the vehicle is moved for repairs.
  • We arrange an inspection and guide you to a network garage if you want cashless repair.
Need help with an ongoing claim? Call our dedicated claims care team at 1800-123-456 (toll-free) or write to claims@securelife.insure with your policy number.
